Our Story

Crafting Moments of Connection

Founded in 2018, Pencraft Studio was born from a simple belief: in our digital world, there's something profoundly meaningful about putting pen to paper.

Our founder, Eliza Bennett, spent years working in the fast-paced tech industry before rediscovering her childhood love for handwritten letters and journaling. What began as a personal passion project quickly evolved into a mission to create stationery products that inspire creativity, mindfulness, and human connection.

Every item in our collection is thoughtfully designed to elevate your writing experience, whether you're journaling your thoughts, sending a heartfelt note, or simply jotting down ideas.

Our Philosophy

At Pencraft Studio, we believe that great stationery is more than just paper and ink—it's a tool for self-expression, reflection, and connection.

We're committed to creating products that are not only beautiful but functional, durable, and sustainably made. We work with skilled artisans and responsible manufacturers who share our values of quality, craftsmanship, and environmental stewardship.

Each item is designed with intention, considering how it feels in your hand, how it inspires your thoughts, and how it will age over time. We believe the best stationery becomes more meaningful with use.

Our Values

Craftsmanship

We partner with skilled artisans who take pride in their work, using techniques that have been refined over generations.

Sustainability

From responsibly sourced materials to eco-friendly packaging, we're committed to minimizing our environmental footprint.

Intentionality

We believe in mindful design that encourages thoughtfulness in both creation and use.

Connection

Our products are designed to help you connect—with yourself through journaling, and with others through written correspondence.
